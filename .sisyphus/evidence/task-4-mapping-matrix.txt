SECTION 6: DEPRECATED place_llm_evidence_markers → DISCARD
================================================================================

This table stores trust markers for LLM output. No target in new schema.

| Deprecated Column  | Decision   | Rationale                                              |
|--------------------|------------|--------------------------------------------------------|
| field_name         | DISCARD    | Evidence markers are process data, not place properties |
| marker_text        | DISCARD    | Trust verification detail not needed in simplified model|
| marker_type        | DISCARD    | Marker classification not needed                        |
| confidence         | DISCARD    | Per-marker confidence not migrated                      |

Rationale: Evidence markers are intermediate trust-verification data used during
enrichment processing. The simplified model captures trust via trust_score in
place_llm_properties. Raw evidence is preserved in place_source_evidence_runs
(which is also deprecated but retained longer for audit purposes).
